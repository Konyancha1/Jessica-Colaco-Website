Colaço

Jessica Colaco is a vibrant bassist with a passion for universe
that is music. Her dream as a teenager was to have a rock band
which she set up in 2016 called BassiiIV. She loves playing Rhythm
and Bass Guitar.

In 2009 - 2010, she played for a professional fusion band called
Akala led by famous Kenyan musician. Viola Karuri and various
other rock bands.

Harnessing the power of her life story, she aims to pour more than
just her heart into the strings, but the message of hope and
redepmtion through life's biggest challenges. The lyrics reminds us
that life may beat us down, but we can always get up.

Her soon to be released EP has the feel of live band performance
that immerses you into her thoughts and feelings as she guides
you through chapter 1 of what has been an epic journey of pain,
growth, and transformation.
